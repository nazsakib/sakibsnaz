Tutor LMS has some excellent features and one of them is integration with WooCommerce. For paid course it's kind of a mandatatory feature of Learning Management Systems. Here's a step by step way to enable the WooCommerce as an ecommerce engine:

**Step 1:**

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360755/settings_tutor_lms_sakibsnaz_hy2mze.webp)

First, from Tutor LMS, click on Settings.

**Step 2:**

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360269/tutor_lms_monetization_sakibsnaz_tutor_lms_integration_with_woocommerce_dk63w9.webp)

From settings, click on Monetization.

**Step 3:**

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360269/search_engine_sakibsnaz_tutor_lms_integration_with_woocommerce_mkic5o.webp)

After clicking Monetization, click on 'Select e-commerce engine'

**Step 4:**

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360269/select_woocommerce_sakibsnaz_tutor_lms_integration_with_woocommerce_pyr8y2.webp)

Click 'Select Option' for expand it and choose 'WooCommerce'. And in top-right corner click 'Save Changes'
You've successfully enable the WooCommerce as an e-commerce search engine for Tutor LMS.

Now you can the 'Add Product' option like below in the 'Course Edit' page:

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715361349/add-product_tutor_lms_course_edit_by_sakibsnaz_s1lkze.webp)

But before you have to 'Add Product' in WooCommerce like below:

- Go to 'Add new' from WooCommerce -> Products

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360268/add-new-product_sakibsnaz_tutor_lms_integration_with_woocommerce_iexhbf.webp)

- Then 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dr1nwz8am/image/upload/v1715360268/course_type_sakibsnaz_tutor_lms_integration_with_woocommerce_idw6bs.webp)

Select 'Virtual' as courses are not meant for home delivery, presented in a beautifully wrapped box. Set the 'Price' and 'Sale Pirce' if applicable.

And done!
